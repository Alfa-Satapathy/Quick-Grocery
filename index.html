Product Description

Our organic apples are grown in orchards using sustainable farming practices. The apples are hand-picked at peak ripeness to ensure the best flavor and nutritional value. We work directly with local farmers who avoid the use of synthetic pesticides and fertilizers, resulting in delicious, crisp apples that are good for both you and the environment.

These versatile apples are excellent for eating fresh, adding to salads, baking into pies, or making homemade applesauce. Their natural sweetness means you can reduce added sugar in your recipes.
